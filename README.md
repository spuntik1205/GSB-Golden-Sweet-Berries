# GSB - Golden Sweet Berries

Ever thought Sweet Berries were a bit underwhelming? Want a shiny, gold-plated snack to take with you on your adventures? This simple, vanilla-friendly mod introduces **Golden Sweet Berries** to Minecraft!

### Features
This mod adds a single, balanced food item to the game:
* **The Golden Sweet Berry:** A luxurious upgrade to the standard sweet berry. 
* **Stats:** Restores **4 Hunger** (2 drumsticks) and provides **11.4 Saturation**. It's slightly less filling than a Golden Carrot, making it a perfectly balanced mid-game food source
* **Crafting:** Crafted just like a Golden Carrot. Surround a single Sweet Berry with 8 Gold Nuggets in a crafting table.

### Recipe
* 1x Sweet Berry (Center)
* 8x Gold Nuggets (Surrounding)
